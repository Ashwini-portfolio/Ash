Learning

Learning keeps my mind engaged and body active.It helps me get new and knowledge-based perspective on the world around me. It helps me gain new experiences, trains my brain to handle a wide range of challenges, and keeps my neural pathways active.

Traveling

Travel exposes us to different cultures and ancient traditions and through these authentic encounters, we learn to embrace and celebrate both our similarities and our differences. Travel teaches us about humanity and gives us an appreciation, understanding and respect for different points of view and ways of life.

Listening to music

Music makes me happier, it provides a total brain workout. Research has shown that listening to music can reduce anxiety, blood pressure, and pain as well as improve sleep quality, mood, mental alertness, and memory.
